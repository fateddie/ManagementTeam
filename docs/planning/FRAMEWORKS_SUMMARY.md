## 🎯 **10 PROFESSIONAL FRAMEWORKS**

### Strategic Analysis:

1. **SWOT Analysis** - Internal strengths/weaknesses + external opportunities/threats
2. **Porter's Five Forces** - Industry attractiveness (5 competitive forces)
3. **PESTLE Analysis** - External macro environment (Political, Economic, Social, Tech, Legal, Environmental)
4. **Ansoff Matrix** - Growth strategy validation
5. **BCG Matrix** - Portfolio classification (Stars, Cash Cows, Question Marks, Dogs)

### Business Model Validation:

6. **Lean Canvas** - 9-box business model validation
7. **Value Proposition Canvas** - Product-market fit assessment
8. **Unit Economics** - LTV:CAC ratio + profitability math

### Risk & Decision Support:

9. **Risk Matrix** - Quantified risk assessment (Probability × Impact)
10. **"So What?" Test** - Critical validation questions (Why now? Why you?)
